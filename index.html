About Us

Pinto's Consulting Group (PCG) is led by Ambar Pinto, a seasoned organizer and fundraiser with over fifteen years of experience working for social justice movements. PCG provides comprehensive, strategic fundraising, grant writing, and development support to organizations of varying sizes and across social justice issues, from immigration to human rights. In the last three years, we have helped clients secure over $3.5M in C3 and C4 funding. Core to PCG's approach is the understanding that effective systems and executive time management are key to setting fundraising goals and executing successful fundraising campaigns. To meet additional client needs (including digital fundraising), PCG leverages a robust network of experts that we engage as subcontractors as needed.

  • Digital Fundraising Strategy
